## 🎨 Theming Specifications

### Brand Name

**DenD**

---

### Primary Colors

*   **Primary:** `#4F46E5` (Indigo 600) - A strong, calming, and modern primary color.
*   **Primary Container:** `#E0E7FF` (Indigo 100) - A light, subtle background for primary elements.
*   **On Primary:** `#FFFFFF`

*   **Secondary:** `#10B981` (Emerald 500) - An energetic, positive color for secondary actions.
*   **Secondary Container:** `#D1FAE5` (Emerald 100)
*   **On Secondary:** `#FFFFFF`

*   **Error:** `#EF4444` (Red 500)
*   **Error Container:** `#FEE2E2` (Red 100)
*   **On Error:** `#FFFFFF`

*   **Background:** `#F9FAFB` (Gray 50) - A very light gray to feel softer than pure white.
*   **Surface:** `#FFFFFF`
*   **On Background/Surface:** `#111827` (Gray 900)

---

### Typography

*   **Font Family:** Roboto Flex or Inter (from Google Fonts)
*   **Weights:** 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 700 (Bold)
*   **Example Usage:**
    *   `Display Small`: `fontSize = 36.sp`, `fontWeight = Bold`
    *   `Headline Medium`: `fontSize = 28.sp`, `fontWeight = Bold`
    *   `Title Large`: `fontSize = 22.sp`, `fontWeight = Medium`
    *   `Body Large`: `fontSize = 16.sp`, `fontWeight = Regular`
    *   `Label Medium`: `fontSize = 12.sp`, `fontWeight = Medium`

---

### Iconography

*   **Icon Pack:** Material Symbols - Rounded
*   **Style:** Consistent stroke weight and filled/unfilled state.
*   **Custom or Specific Icons:**
    *   **Zen Mode:** `ic_zen_mode` or `self_improvement` (lotus flower)
    *   **Blacklist:** `gpp_bad` or `block`
    *   **Whitelist:** `verified_user` or `task_alt`
    *   **Firewall:** `security` or `shield`
    *   **Privacy:** `privacy_tip`

---

### Component Style

*   **Buttons:** Rounded corners (e.g., `24.dp`) for a modern, friendly feel.
*   **Cards:** Slight elevation with soft shadows.
*   **Switches/Toggles:** Use primary color when active to provide clear visual feedback.
